FAQ

Common questions about cleaning in Cyprus. If you need more details, please contact us.

How long does cleaning take?Timing depends on the size and type of cleaning. We confirm a time estimate before the visit.
What is included in regular cleaning?Dry and wet cleaning of accessible surfaces, floors, and standard bathroom cleaning without heavy stain removal.
What is not included in the price?Hard‑to‑reach areas, stubborn stains, deep upholstery cleaning, and high window cleaning are add‑ons.
Can I order additional services?Yes. Add‑ons like oven, fridge, or windows are available upon agreement.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?Not necessarily. You can provide access and we’ll coordinate details in advance.
What cleaning products do you use?We use professional products safe for surfaces. If needed, we can use your products.
How is the price calculated?Pricing depends on the cleaning type and number of rooms. The final cost is confirmed before work starts.
Do you offer after‑renovation cleaning?Yes, there is a dedicated tariff for construction dust and residues.
Can I reschedule or cancel?Yes, please let us know in advance and we will reschedule.
What if something is missed?Let us know as soon as possible and we’ll review and propose a solution.
